Overview


Visa Seekers entering UAE are required to have a valid national passport with a valid visa obtained from an UAE Embassy board. You are requested to apply for the UAE Visa through Online application link .

You can apply for a visa if you want to visit the UAE:

  • for leisure, for example on holiday or to see your family and friends
  • for business, or to take part in sports or creative events
  • for another reason, for example to receive private medical treatment
